For a long time this site was a single page: my name, my title, a couple of
links. It did its job as a calling card, but it was never a place to actually
*show the work* — and lately I've been doing work worth showing.

So I rebuilt it. Same look and feel, but now there's room to grow:

- **A blog** (you're reading it) for documenting what I'm building and learning.
- **Projects** — the homelab and infrastructure work I do outside the day job.
- **A resume** that stays current instead of living in a PDF on my desktop.

## What I'll write about

Mostly the things I spend my evenings on: running Kubernetes at home, GitOps
workflows, Linux automation with Ansible and Puppet, and the occasional war
story from administering production systems. If it taught me something, it's
probably worth a post.

More soon.
