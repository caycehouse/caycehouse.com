The site you're looking at. Statically generated with **Astro** and styled with
**Tailwind CSS v4**, type-checked on every build, and deployed to **Cloudflare
Workers** static assets.

It's intentionally small and fast, with a content-collection-backed blog and
projects section so it's easy to keep current — and a Konami-code easter egg,
because why not.
